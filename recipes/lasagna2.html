Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possible one of the oldest types made of very wide, flat sheets. Refers to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragu, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and seasonings and spices, like Italian seasoning, such as garlic, oregano and basil

The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted after baking. Typically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in the oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square portions.
